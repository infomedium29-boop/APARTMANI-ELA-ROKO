Privacy Policy

This policy explains which personal data is collected through the Apartments Ela & Roko website and how it is used.

Last updated: 22 June 2026

Data controller

Apartments Ela & Roko, Cvijetno naselje 2/a, 20260 Korčula, Croatia. Contact: roko.korcula@gmail.com, phone: +385 91 51 52 555.

Data we collect

When you send a reservation inquiry, we may collect your full name, e-mail address, phone number, number of guests, preferred apartment, arrival date, departure date and any additional message you choose to provide.

Purpose of processing

We use this data only to reply to your inquiry, check apartment availability, communicate with you and organize a possible stay.

Legal basis

Processing is based on steps taken at the guest’s request before a possible reservation, legitimate interest in user communication and, where applicable, consent for optional cookies.

Recipients

Form data is sent to the owner’s e-mail inbox. A third-party form delivery service may be used for technical e-mail delivery. Data is not sold to third parties.

Retention

Inquiry data is kept only as long as needed for communication about the inquiry or reservation, unless legal obligations require longer retention.

Your rights

You may request access, correction, deletion, restriction of processing, data portability and object to processing. To exercise your rights, contact us by e-mail.

Cookies

Details about cookies are available on the Cookie Policy page.

Note

This is a practical informational version for an accommodation website. For full legal compliance, review by a legal professional is recommended.

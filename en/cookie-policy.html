What are cookies?

Cookies are small files or records used by websites to remember certain information about your visit. This website uses browser local storage to remember your cookie choice.

Cookie categories

Necessary cookies: required for website operation and storing your consent choice. They cannot be disabled through the banner.

Analytics cookies: may be used to measure visits and website usage. They are currently inactive until analytics are added.

Marketing cookies: may be used for advertising or remarketing. They are currently inactive until marketing tools are added.

Managing consent

On your first visit, you can accept all, reject optional cookies or save custom settings. You can reopen settings by clicking “Cookie settings” in the footer.

External services

The inquiry form may use an external service for e-mail delivery. Clicking WhatsApp, Viber, Google Maps or e-mail may open external apps or services with their own privacy policies.
